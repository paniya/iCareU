Lab Tests

We have facilities to do laboratory tests like testing samples of blood, urine, or other tissues or substances in the body. Our laboratory services are a vital component of quick and accurate diagnosis, and subsequent treatment. Doctors will use these tests to identify changes in your health condition before any symptoms occur or diagnose a disease or condition before you have symptoms and also to plan your treatment for a disease or condition by monitoring the course of a disease over time.
